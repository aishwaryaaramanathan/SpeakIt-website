ABOUT US

India has over 6.3 crore hearing-impaired individuals with limited access to speech therapy due to their expensive cost.

SpeakIt - Speech Coach for the Deaf is a browser-based tool that teaches your hearing impaired children to speak through visual - audio feedback. It offers low-cost, scalable support at zero recurring cost to our users.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make speech therapy accessible to everyone, regardless of their economic status or location. We believe that communication is a fundamental right, and we are committed to helping hearing-impaired individuals develop their speaking skills.

How It Works

SpeakIt uses advanced technology to provide real-time visual feedback on pronunciation. Users can see how their mouth should move to form different sounds, and receive immediate feedback on their attempts. This visual-audio approach is particularly effective for those who rely on visual cues for learning.

Our Team

We are a dedicated team of speech therapists, software developers, and accessibility advocates working together to create innovative solutions for the hearing-impaired community.
