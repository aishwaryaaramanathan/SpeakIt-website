FAQ

Do you have any questions for us?

If there are question you want to ask. We will answer all your question

01

Is it available for languages other than English?

Not yet, we are working on it to bring it you soon.

02

Do we need any external hardware?

A simple headphone would suffice. If in case you don't have one don't sweat it. Make sure to have a quietest environment.

03

How accurate is the feedback?

Our system provides feedback with approximately 85-90% accuracy. The accuracy improves over time as our AI learns from more users and continues to be trained with diverse speech patterns.

04

Can adults use SpeakIt too?

While SpeakIt was initially designed with children in mind, it's equally effective for adults who are learning to speak or improving their pronunciation.

05

Is there a mobile app available?

Currently, SpeakIt is a browser-based tool that works on any device with a modern web browser. We're working on dedicated mobile apps that will be available in the future.
